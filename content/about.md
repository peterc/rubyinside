*Ruby Inside* was one of the most widely-read Ruby blogs from 2006 to 2012, covering the Ruby programming language, its ecosystem, and the community that grew around it. It has been relaunched in 2026 as a venue for new articles, news updates, and more about Ruby and Rails.

The content from 2006 to 2012 has been imported and can be found in [our archives.](http://localhost:1313/archives/) Please note that this content is largely out of date and maintained solely as a 'time capsule' of what was going on in Ruby long ago. The [old 'About' page on the Wayback Archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20110225012505/http://www.rubyinside.com/about) provides more information about the original site.

Ruby Inside is published by Peter Cooper, editor of [Ruby Weekly.](https://rubyweekly.com/)
