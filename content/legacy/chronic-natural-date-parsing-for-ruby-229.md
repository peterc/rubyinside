<p><a href="http://chronic.rubyforge.org/">Chronic</a> is a natural language (English only, at present, I think) time and date parser written entirely in Ruby. It supports a staggering number of different ways of expressing the date and time. For example:</p>
<blockquote><p><em>tomorrow<br>
this monday 07:30<br>
may 15th<br>
tomorrow morning<br>
yesterday at 9:00<br>
tomorrow at 11pm<br>
wednesday last week</em></p></blockquote>
<p>Time.parse already does a great job, but this goes a step further. You can install chronic quickly with <em>gem install chronic</em>.</p>
