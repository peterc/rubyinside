<p>The wonderful people over at DZone (a Digg-like site for developers) are expanding their online publishing empire with a step into the Ruby community. The new site is tentatively named RubyZone and DZone is <a href="http://jobs.dzone.com/id/1">looking to hire an editor-in-chief</a> and <a href="http://jobs.dzone.com/id/2">a couple of contributing editors</a>. If you're a good writer and/or editor and you want to make a bigger name for yourself in the Ruby community, this is a great stepping stone. In my experience, the guys at DZone are incredibly nice too, so don't be afraid to get in touch!</p>
