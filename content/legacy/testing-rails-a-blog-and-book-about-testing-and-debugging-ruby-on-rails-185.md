<p>Evan Henshaw-Plath (more commonly known as <em>rabble</em>) is in the process of writing a book for O'Reilly about testing and debugging Ruby on Rails applications, and has just launched a companion blog, <a href="http://blog.testingrails.com/">Testing Rails</a>. The subject of the blog is exactly what the title says, and <em>rabble</em> hopes to post at least one in-depth tutorial relating to Rails and testing each week. The first is <a href="http://blog.testingrails.com/2006/8/7/building-tests-from-logs-test-driven-debugging">Building Tests from Logs - Test Driven Debugging</a>.</p>
