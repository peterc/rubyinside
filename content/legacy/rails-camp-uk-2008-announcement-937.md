<p>Following on from the <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/railscamp08">outstanding success</a> of the "Rails Camp" events that have taken place in Australia, comes <a href="http://railscamp08.org/">Rails Camp UK</a>, the equivalent for Brits (and brave travellers). The first is from Friday, August 15th to Monday, August 18th and takes place just a 30 minute train ride away from London, in Orpington, Kent (my teenage stomping grounds, as it happens).</p>
<p>The organizers say that they want to get together a "posse of like-minded Ruby hackers" to hack and have fun together in an open environment. The whole weekend costs £45 (and includes food and basic accommodation) with a day pass available for £20. Places are limited. You can <a href="http://railscampuk.eventwax.com/rails-camp-uk/register">sign up on EventWax</a> and a <a href="http://groups.google.com/group/railscamp-uk">Google group has been put together</a> for attendees to discuss the event.</p>
