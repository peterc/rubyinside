<blockquote class="stylized"><p>I'm pleased to announce that we released DataMapper 1.0 "Vermouth" earlier today.</p></blockquote>
<p><cite>Dan Kubb</cite></p>
<p><a href="http://datamapper.org/">DataMapper</a>, one of the best known Ruby ORMs (Object Relational Mapper), has reached a significant landmark: version 1.0. DataMapper is typically competing against ActiveRecord and <a href="http://sequel.rubyforge.org/">Sequel</a> amongst Ruby developers who want to talk to databases, but DM offers <a href="http://datamapper.org/why">a number of unique advantages</a> and is extremely flexible and modular (even supporting <a href="http://redis.io/">Redis</a>-based stores).</p>
