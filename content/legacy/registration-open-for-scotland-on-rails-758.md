<p>The <a href="http://scotlandonrails.com/register">registration process</a> for <a href="http://scotlandonrails.com/">Scotland on Rails</a>, the UK's first Rails-dedicated conference, has gone live. The event takes place in Edinburgh, Scotland on Friday 4th and Saturday 5th April, and comes just days after both <a href="http://www.euruko2008.org/">Euruko 2008</a> (Czech Republic) and <a href="http://jaoo.dk/ruby-cph/conference/">Ruby Fools</a> (Denmark &amp; Norway), so might help you form the perfect trifecta! Registration is £180 (€241 or $350).</p>
<p>For a regional conference, Scotland on Rails has done extremely well on <a href="http://scotlandonrails.com/speakers">the speaker front</a>, with Michael Koziarski, David Black, Giles Bowkett, Martin Sadler, Tammer Saleh, Jim Weirich and Bruce Williams speaking amongst others. A <a href="http://scotlandonrails.com/talks">list of talks</a> is available for your perusal.</p>
