<p>The team behind <a href="http://rspec.info/">RSpec</a>, a Behavior-Driven Development based "testing" library, have <a href="http://blog.davidchelimsky.net/articles/2007/12/14/rspec-1-1">announced the release</a> of RSpec 1.1.0. This will be of particular interest to Rails 2.0 developers as support has now been added, along with interoperability with Test::Unit. RSpec 1.1 also includes a Rails tool called "RailsStory" that allows you write "user stories" that can be tested out on the fly.</p>
