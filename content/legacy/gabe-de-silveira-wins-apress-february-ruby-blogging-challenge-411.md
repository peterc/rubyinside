<p>A month ago, Pat Eyler (<a href="http://on-ruby.blogspot.com/">On Ruby</a>), <a href="http://www.apress.com/">Apress</a>, and I <a href="how-has-ruby-blown-your-mind-blog-your-answer-and-win-375.html">launched a Ruby blogging challenge</a> with the question, "<a href="http://on-ruby.blogspot.com/2007/01/blogging-contest-february-challenge.html">How Has Ruby Blown Your Mind?</a>" .. There were <a href="http://on-ruby.blogspot.com/2007/01/blogging-contest-february-challenge.html">18 solid entries</a>, and one <a href="http://www.seanhussey.com/2007/2/28/my-ruby-wow-moments">late entry</a> by Sean Hussey that I think would have won if it hadn't come late. The eventual winner was <a href="http://darwinweb.net/article/Ruby_Blocks_As_Closures">Ruby Blocks as Closures</a> by Gabe de Silveira, and he wins three Apress books of his choosing. Well done Gabe!</p>
