<p>Ian Ozsvald wrote to tell me that <a href="http://showmedo.com/">ShowMeDo</a> now features a <a href="http://showmedo.com/videos/series?name=fXRfVLC1J">new series of introductory Ruby videos by Chinmoy Gavini</a>. There are currently five videos in the series and they start off with a basic walkthrough of irb and Ruby's basic features, moving on to topics such as creating and using classes, deep copy versus shallow copy, regular expressions, marshaling, and CSV.</p>
