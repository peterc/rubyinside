<p>Florian Hanke got in touch to tell me that presentations and photos from the recent <a href="http://euruko2010.org/">Euruko 2010</a> European Ruby conference are now online.</p>
<p><a href="http://euruko2010.heroku.com/">http://euruko2010.heroku.com/</a> shows off the presentations awesomely! Videos and slides are available for most of them and the video/audio is of a high quality.</p>
<div class="img"><a href="http://euruko2010.heroku.com/"><img src="/assets/2010/07/00011.png" alt="" title="0001" width="460" height="451" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3539"></a></div>
<p>Highlights include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="http://vimeo.com/12609430">Continuous Integration and Why You Need It</a> — Nicolas (foca) Sanguinetti</li>
<li>
<a href="http://vimeo.com/12615297">Getting and loading code: current state of packaging &amp; best practices</a> — Mislav Marohnić</li>
<li>
<a href="http://euruko2010.heroku.com/Florian-Hanke-">Building a search engine with Ruby</a> — Florian Hanke</li>
<li>
<a href="http://euruko2010.heroku.com/Yehuda-Katz-">Why Bundler?</a> — Yehuda Katz</li>
<li>
<a href="http://euruko2010.heroku.com/Tim-Lossen-">Remodelling a Facebook game</a> — Tim Lossen</li>
<li>
<a href="http://vimeo.com/12666052">Putting the static back into Ruby</a> — Tomasz Stachewicz</li>
<li>
<a href="http://euruko2010.heroku.com/Sven-Fuchs-">Anatomy of Ruby i18n</a> — Sven Fuchs</li>
<li>
<a href="http://vimeo.com/12674501">Matz's Keynote</a> — Matz!</li>
</ul>
<p>More can be <a href="http://vimeo.com/tag:euruko">found on Vimeo</a> and if you'd rather just read a write up of what happened, Markus Prinz has writeups for <a href="http://nuclearsquid.com/writings/euruko-day1.html">Day 1</a> and <a href="http://nuclearsquid.com/writings/euruko-day2.html">Day 2</a> of the conference.</p>
