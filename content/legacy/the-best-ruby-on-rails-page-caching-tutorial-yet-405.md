<p>Just ten days ago Gregg Pollack and Jason Seifer launched their new Rails blog, <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/">Rails Envy</a>, with a <a href="in-depth-acts_as_ferret-tutorial-add-search-to-your-rails-app-391.html">great acts_as_ferret tutorial</a>. I told them to keep up the good work, and they have! Today they've posted <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/2007/2/28/rails-caching-tutorial">an amazing Ruby on Rails page caching tutorial</a> that covers page caching to a good depth including configuration, sweepers, cache location, and Apache/Lighttpd integration. Sadly it doesn't touch on fragment and action caching, but Gregg assures us all that a post about these tools is forthcoming.</p>
