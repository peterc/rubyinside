<p>Steve Odom writes about <a href="http://niblets.wordpress.com/2007/02/12/capistrano-ec2-sitting-in-a-tree-k-i-s-s-i-n-g/">how he uses Capistrano and Amazon's Elastic Compute Cloud</a> to deploy a Rails application to a fresh server within minutes. He includes the scripts and techniques he uses.</p>
<p>This article makes me think of the possibilities of using Pound and perhaps even a database outside of EC2 and then seamlessly deploying Rails instances within EC2 and letting Pound do the balancing. The infrastructure problem is getting easier every day it seems.</p>
