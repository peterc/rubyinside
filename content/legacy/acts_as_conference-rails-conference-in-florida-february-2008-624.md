<p><a href="http://www.actsasconference.com/">acts_as_conference</a> is a Rails conference taking place in Orlando, Florida on February 8 and 9, 2008, and a pretty good excuse for getting out of the cold weather up north for a couple of days. It's currently early days in the organization of the conference, so no speakers have been announced and a call for proposals is due soon. The conference is being organized by Robert Dempsey, also known for his work on <a href="http://www.railsforall.org/">Rails For All</a> and <a href="http://www.techcfl.com/">Atlantic Dominion Solutions</a>.</p>
