<p>Just a quick newsflash..</p>
<blockquote><p>Coming up fast, the deadline for submitting proposals for sessions and tutorials at <a href="http://www.railsconfeurope.com/">RailsConf Europe 2007</a> is Monday, 2 April. Co-produced by Ruby Central and O'Reilly Media, this is the largest European conference dedicated to all things Ruby on Rails. The three-day program will include revelatory keynotes, informative sessions, and hands-on tutorials, as well as plenty of opportunity to connect, contribute, and collaborate.</p></blockquote>
<p>Learn more at the <a href="http://www.railsconfeurope.com/">official RailsConf Europe 2007 site</a>.</p>
