<p>Manuel Holtgrewe presents <em><a href="http://www.bitwiese.de/2007/09/on-processes-and-threads.html">Ruby's Multithreading: On Processes and Threads</a></em>, a great look at the processes (pun intended) involved with Ruby's threading system and the difference between kernel level threads and pure user level threads. He then makes an argument why a process oriented system for division of labor can make more sense than a thread oriented one.</p>
