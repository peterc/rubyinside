<p>Promoting companies is not going to become a regular thing on Ruby Inside, but <a href="http://guruza.com/">Guruza</a> struck me as quite interesting. It's a Google Answers style site but in a Web 2.0 vein. Interestingly, though, there are quite a few people asking and answering Ruby questions on there (whereas I've found other questions sites to be thin on programming stuff). Check out the <a href="http://guruza.com/question/latest/ruby/all/1">latest Ruby questions</a>. People asking questions specify an amount in dollars of how much they'd pay for an answer, and if you satisfactorily answer the question, the money is yours.</p>
<p>If the smell of money makes your head spin, then there's always <a href="http://rails.techno-weenie.net/">Rails Weenie</a>, a pretty amazing, free, Rails questions and answers system managed by Rails god Rick Olson.</p>
