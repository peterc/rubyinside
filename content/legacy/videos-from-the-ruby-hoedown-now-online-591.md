<p>The <a href="http://www.rubyhoedown.com/">Ruby Hoedown</a> was a North Carolina based Ruby conference that took place on August 10-11, 2007. From all accounts, it was a roaring success, and now the <a href="http://rubyhoedown2007.confreaks.com/">videos of the presentations given have been made available</a>. Topics include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<em>Ruby and Rails Testing Techniques</em> by Marcel Molina, Bruce Tate, and Chad Fowler.</li>
<li>
<em>Exploring Merb</em> by Ezra Zygmuntowicz.</li>
<li>
<em>Next-Gen VoIP Development with Ruby and Adhearsion</em> by Jay Phillips.</li>
<li>
<em>Building Games with Ruby</em> by Andrea O.K. Wright.</li>
<li>
<em>Does Ruby Have a Chasm to Cross?</em> by Ken Auer.</li>
<li>
<em>Using C to Tune Your Ruby or Rails Application</em> by Jared Richardson.</li>
</ul>
<p>All this along with some lightning talks and two keynotes by Bruce Tate and Marcel Molina. Enjoy!</p>
