<p>Back in February, I did a <a href="review-of-build-your-own-ruby-on-rails-applications-by-patrick-lenz-402.html">brief review of "Build Your Own Ruby on Rails Applications"</a> written by Patrick Lenz and published by SitePoint. For my impressions of the book, refer to the review, but my opinion of the book was generally good, especially for newer Rails developers. Now SitePoint has <a href="http://www.sitepoint.com/books/rails1/freebook.php">made the book freely available in PDF format</a> for the next two months, so if you didn't get the book the first time around, check it out!</p>
