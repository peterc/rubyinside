<p>From the ever-productive Erik Veenstra comes <a href="http://www.erikveen.dds.nl/allinoneruby/index.html">AllInOneRuby</a>, a "<em>Just-in-Time and Temporary Installation of Ruby</em>".  The concept is simple:</p>
<blockquote><p><em>AllInOneRuby creates a compressed executable for Windows, Linux or Mac OS X (Darwin) that includes both the Ruby interpreter and the runtime libraries. Why? Because it's sometimes not easy, or possible, or desirable, or allowed to do a complete Ruby installation. That's where AllInOneRuby comes in. I always have a USB-memory stick with AllInOneRuby in my pocket.</em></p></blockquote>
<p>I need to try this along with Platypus, and see if I can deliver a complete working Rails application as a 'click and run' application for regular users on OS X (and Windows, though not with Platypus).</p>
