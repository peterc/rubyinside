<p><a href="/assets/2009/05/images1.jpeg"><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1800" src="/assets/2009/05/images1.jpeg" alt="" width="111" height="91"></a><span>Did you miss the <a href="http://scotlandonrails.com/">Scotland on Rails</a> conference this year? No need to fret though, as Engine Yard are hosting videos of <a href="http://www.engineyard.com/blog/community/scotland-on-rails/">all the presentations</a> made at this popular conference. With 27 presentations covering topics from deployment to testing, there is something here for everyone.</span></p>
<p>This is an awesome way to "catch up" with the spirit of what's been going on at some of the recent conferences, especially as a few of the talks have been given elsewhere too (such as RailsConf). Enjoy!</p>
