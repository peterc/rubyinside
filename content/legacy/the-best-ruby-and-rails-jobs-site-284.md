I don't think the <a href="http://jobs.rubynow.com/">Ruby Jobs site at RubyNow</a> gets enough link love from the community, and I want to put that right. If you haven't been there for a while, you'll be surprised. In just the last two weeks there are 30 new Ruby and Rails jobs available. That's even more than on <a href="http://jobs.37signals.com/categories/2/jobs">the 37signals Jobs Board</a>. Even better, if you're an employer you can post to the RubyNow jobs board for free, so check it out.</p>
