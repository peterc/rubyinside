<p>At RailsConf 2010, Fabio Akita (of <a href="http://akitaonrails.com/">AkitaOnRails.com</a>) went crazy with his camcorder and interviewed a wide selection of Rubyists, as well as famous C++ and Agile manifesto developer <a href="http://www.objectmentor.com/omTeam/martin_r.html">Bob Martin.</a></p>
<p>The interviews are divided up into groups:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="http://www.akitaonrails.com/2010/06/16/railsconf-2010-video-interview-robert-martin-english">Robert Martin</a> - as well as an interview with Fabio, a video of his keynote at RailsConf 2010 is also included</li>
<li>
<a href="http://www.akitaonrails.com/2010/06/17/railsconf-2010-video-interview-david-hansson">David Heinemeier Hansson</a> - 23 minutes of one-on-one with DHH - a refreshing change from his usual keynote/mass audience style</li>
<li>
<a href="http://www.akitaonrails.com/2010/06/17/railsconf-2010-video-interviews-part-1">Miscellaneous interviews</a> - covering Ryan Bates (Railscasts), Ben Scofield, James Golick, Carl Lerche, and Santiago Pastorino.</li>
<li>
<a href="http://www.akitaonrails.com/2010/06/17/railsconf-2010-video-interviews-part-2">Ruby Heroes winner interviews</a> - covering Aaron Patterson [Nokogiri], Gregory Brown [Prawn], Nick Quaranto [Gemcutter], and Wayne E Seguin [RVM]</li>
</ul>
