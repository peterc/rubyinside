You might recall, a few short months ago, that Dr. Nic <a href="myconfplan-plan-your-railsconf-2007-schedule-now-468.html">built and released a site</a> called MyConfPlan. Its prime objective was to enable RailsConf 2007 visitors to easily plan their schedules, although it had support for other events too. <a href="http://drnicwilliams.com/2007/08/23/myconfplan-for-sale/">Dr. Nic has now put the site up for sale on eBay</a>, and is donating all of the proceeds to charity as part of his pledge to donate to charity for RailsConf Europe. The <a href="http://cgi.ebay.com.au/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?ViewItem&amp;item=160150359140">auction</a> ends in the next couple of days and is already at $470 Australian dollars (about <span style="text-decoration:line-through;">$42</span> $386 US Dollars).</p>
