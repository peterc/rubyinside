<p><a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/2007/3/22/ruby-on-rails-video-presentation">"Why Ruby On Rails?"</a> was a presentation made by Gregg Pollack at the Orlando Ruby Users Group on March 15, and now you can watch it online. Gregg is a great presenter and moves through a lot of stuff really quickly. The first half of the presentation focuses on agility, agile development, and how agile development changes the way you need to interact with clients, and the second half gets into the technical Rails side of things. A good watch, and especially good to recommend to management types who, perhaps, aren't too familiar with Rails and agility yet.</p>
