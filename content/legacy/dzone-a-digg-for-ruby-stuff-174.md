<p>Okay, the title is a little white lie. <a href="http://www.dzone.com/">DZone</a> is actually a Digg-like site covering <em>all</em> programming and development topics, but recently <a href="http://www.dzone.com/links/tag/ruby.html">its Ruby section</a> has become more popular than when I first checked it out a few months ago. With its focus on developers, DZone has a great feel, and I personally feel many of the features are better than those offered by Digg. If you have a Ruby article to promote, give it a try.</p>
