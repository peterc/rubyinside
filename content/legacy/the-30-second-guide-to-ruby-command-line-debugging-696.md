<p>Chad Woolley has put together a helpful blog post called "<a href="http://pivots.pivotallabs.com/users/chad/blog/articles/366-ruby-debug-in-30-seconds-we-don-t-need-no-stinkin-gui-">ruby-debug in 30 seconds (we don't need no stinkin' GUI!)</a>" that covers how to use the ruby-debug command line debugger to debug Ruby applications. Also of interest will be <a href="http://cheat.errtheblog.com/s/rdebug/">this cheat sheet</a> by the guys over at <a href="http://errfree.com/">ErrFree</a>.</p>
<p>If you're not into GUI editors or haven't found a GUI Ruby debugging solution, it's worth looking into <a href="http://www.datanoise.com/ruby-debug/">ruby-debug</a>. Chad says now that he actually prefer this "IRB-style" of debugging to the graphical alternatives.</p>
