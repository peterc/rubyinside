<p>The <a href="http://www.railsforum.com/">Rails Forum</a> has announced a Rails Tutorial contest:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>To celebrate our 500 member mark obtained yesterday, and in the interest of getting more good Ruby on Rails tutorials, Rails Forum is giving away an iPod shuffle to the best tutorial written between now and 12:01 AM GMT on November 1, 2006!</em></p>
<p>What do I need to do to enter?</p>
<p>Simple! Just post a tutorial in our <em><a href="http://railsforum.com/viewforum.php?id=20">Ruby on Rails Tutorials forum</a></em><em> between now and the time above, and you'll be automatically entered!</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>To learn more, read <a href="http://www.railsforum.com/viewtopic.php?id=654">the announcement post</a>. If any good tutorials end up on there, I'll be posting references to them here on Ruby Inside.. so even if you don't enter, there should be something to get out of it :)</p>
