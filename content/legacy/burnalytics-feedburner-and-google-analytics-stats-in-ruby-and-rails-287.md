<a href="http://www.eribium.org/?p=51">Burnalytics</a> is a new library just released by Alex MacCaw (the developer also behind the popular <a href="http://www.eribium.org/?cat=3">Juggernaut</a> plugin for Rails) that makes it easy to retrieve statistics from both <a href="http://www.feedburner.com/">FeedBurner</a> and Google Analytics. As well as making it easy to retrieve statistics from these two sources, it also makes it a one line operation to produce graphs from the data retrieved as demonstrated by the screenshot above.</p>
