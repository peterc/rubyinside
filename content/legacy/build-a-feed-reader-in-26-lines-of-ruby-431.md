<p>Ever thought that parsing feeds was a complex task? Not in Ruby! Using the <a href="http://feed-normalizer.rubyforge.org/">Feed Normalizer</a> library (a wrapper for other feed processing libraries), Ilya Grigorik demonstrates <a href="http://www.igvita.com/blog/2007/03/22/agile-rss-aggregator-in-ruby/">how you can create a complete RSS / Atom feed aggregator in 26 lines of code</a>, including renderer!</p>
