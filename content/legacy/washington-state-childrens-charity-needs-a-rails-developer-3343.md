<p>I received this e-mail today:</p>
<blockquote class="stylized">
<p>I work at a small non-profit that helps destitute children in Sierra Leone, Malawi, Uganda and the Dominican Republic (<a href="http://www.cotni.org/">Children of the Nations</a>). We are <a href="http://www.cotni.org/jobs/12">searching for a RoR developer</a> and money is tight.</p>
<p>Would you be willing to list our job opening pro bono? The extra visibility could make the difference as we are searching for a very special person.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><cite>Aaron Stroud</cite></p>
<p>Who could refuse such a short, pleasant e-mail? Not me, which is why if you're looking to soothe your soul and do some Rails work, you should check out <a href="http://www.cotni.org/jobs/12">their job listing.</a> You need to be near to Seattle as you need to be on-site at least a day or two each week, but full-time <em>and</em> half-time arrangements will be considered.</p>
