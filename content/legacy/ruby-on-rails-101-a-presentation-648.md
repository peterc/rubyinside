<p>Rails developer <a href="http://marklunds.com/">Peter Marklund</a> recently presented a five day "Rails 101" course in Sweden, and used an epic 341 slide presentation to work his way through all of the topics. Inspired by the vast number of Rails videos, screencasts, and educational resources coming online lately, Peter decided to <a href="http://marklunds.com/articles/one/374">make his slideshow available to all</a> (direct links: <a href="http://marklunds.com/rails_101.pdf">PDF</a> or <a href="http://www.slideshare.net/peter_marklund/ruby-on-rails-101-presentation-slides-for-a-five-day-introductory-course">Slideshare</a>). Topics include everything you'd expect from a comprehensive Rails course (installation, Ruby, migrations, ActiveRecord, forms, views, Rails 2.0, deployment, etc).</p>
