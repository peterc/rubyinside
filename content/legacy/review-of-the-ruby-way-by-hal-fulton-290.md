<p>In 2001 Hal Fulton released <em>The Ruby Way</em>, only the second English language Ruby book available. Since then Ruby has become popular in the West and with the help of <a href="http://www.rubyonrails.com/">Ruby on Rails</a> has achieved significant critical success. With a significant number of enhancements and changes to the Ruby language and ecosystem of libraries and developers, Fulton decided a second edition was in order. That second edition of <em>The Ruby Way</em>, as published by <a href="http://www.awprofessional.com/">Addison-Wesley</a>, is now available.</p>
