<p>The <a href="http://www.rubyhitsquad.com/About_the_Hit_Squad.html">Ruby Hit Squad</a> is a crack team of Ruby developers - formed of Ryan Davis, Eric Hodel, Wilson Bilkovich, and Shaners Becker - devoted to eliminating "needless complexity." Its first product, <a href="http://www.rubyhitsquad.com/Vlad_the_Deployer.html">Vlad the Deployer</a>, is a <a href="http://www.capify.org/">Capistrano</a> competitor, designed to provide a fast, uncomplicated remote application deployment tool, but without the supposed complexity Capistrano offers. This is a top team of developers, and this is a big first splash, so check it out because it's bound to become popular in the Ruby world.</p>
