<p>Ruby Inside is <a href="win-100-by-blogging-about-ruby-or-rails-this-week-184.html">running a $100 contest for people who write</a> a Ruby or Rails related article, resource post, or tutorial this week. There's still just over 24 hours left to run so get your entries in quickly if you want a chance at winning the $100.  So far there have been 16 entries and they're all pretty good. But.. there are <strong>only two entries for the second prize so far</strong>, so if you want some great odds at winning the $15 second prize, link to <a href="win-100-by-blogging-about-ruby-or-rails-this-week-184.html">the contest</a> and let us know about it!</p>
