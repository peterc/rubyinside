<p>While there have been attempts at porting Ruby to the Symbian (mobile devices) platform before, Symbian have now <a href="http://developer.symbian.com/main/tools/opensrc/ruby/index.jsp">released an official build of Ruby for Symbian OS</a> (S60 to be precise). This brings Ruby to a vast universe of cellphones. Symbian are providing the project with libraries for rendering, messaging, and persistence on their platforms.</p>
