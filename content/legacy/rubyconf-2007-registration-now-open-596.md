<p>The registration process for <a href="http://www.rubyconf.org/">RubyConf 2007</a> is <a href="http://chadfowler.com/2007/9/6/rubyconf-registration-is-open">now open!</a> The conference is due to be held at the Omni Charlotte Hotel in Charlotte, North Carolina in the United States between November 2 - 4 (Friday through Sunday). The official site hasn't been updated yet (as of Friday September 7) but <a href="http://www.regonline.com/Checkin.asp?EventId=145820">the registration form is available here</a>. Tickets are $250.</p>
