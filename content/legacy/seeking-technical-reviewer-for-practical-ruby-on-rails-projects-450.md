<p>Jason Gilmore, open source editor at Apress, is looking for a technical reviewer to help out with a book called "Practical Ruby on Rails Projects". Don't quote me on this, but I believe it's a paid role, and one that will garner a nice page and photo in the book (as it did for the technical reviewers on "Beginning Ruby"). Some Rails experience is required, even if it's just on personal projects.</p>
<p>You can contact Jason directly at <em>jason -/at/- apress.com</em> if you're interested. To make his life easier, it'd probably be good if you could state who you are, what your Rails experience is, and so forth.</p>
