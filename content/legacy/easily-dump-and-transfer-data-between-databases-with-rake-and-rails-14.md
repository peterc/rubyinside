<p><a href="http://blog.leetsoft.com/">Tobias Lütke</a> needed to migrate a database from one architecture to another but needed to copy the data across intact. To ensure that the format would be architecture agnostic, he's created <a href="http://blog.leetsoft.com/2006/5/29/easy-migration-between-databases">a plugin that dumps the data to YAML</a> and then reloads it back into the database at the other end. It's a simple rake task, and Tobi gives <a href="http://blog.leetsoft.com/2006/5/29/easy-migration-between-databases">quick and easy instructions</a>.</p>
