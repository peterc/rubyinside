<p>Josh Susser is <a href="http://blog.hasmanythrough.com/2007/1/26/rfp-silicon-valley-ruby-conf-2007">reporting</a> on <a href="http://groups.google.com/group/san-francisco-ruby/browse_thread/thread/aab6c5197d8e1797?hl=en">Mark Carey's announcement</a> that the "<a href="http://www.sdforum.org/">SDForum</a> Ruby Conference" (informally known as the Silicon Valley Ruby Conference) is now requesting papers for the conference taking place between April 21-22, 2007. Interested parties can submit their proposals to <em>proposals2007 [at] rubysf [dot] net</em>. All proposals submitted before February 4, 2007 will be given consideration, and those submitted after then may not, so if you want a chance to give a presentation, you need to be quick!</p>
