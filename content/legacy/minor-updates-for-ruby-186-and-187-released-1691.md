<p>Over on the <a href="http://www.ruby-lang.org/en/news/">official Ruby news</a> site, Urabe Shyouhei has <a href="http://www.ruby-lang.org/en/news/2009/04/18/ruby-1-8-7-p160-and-1-8-6-p368-released/">announced</a> the release of minor updates to both Ruby 1.8.6 and 1.8.7, namely 1.8.6p368 and 1.8.7p160:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Updates to already-released Ruby 1.8.7 and 1.8.6 have been released.</p>
<p>This time we have fixed dozens of bugs, including workarounds for CVE-2007-1558 and CVE-2008-1447. Many segfaults are also fixed. For a complete list of what has been fixed, please read the ChangeLogs (<a href="http://svn.ruby-lang.org/repos/ruby/tags/v1_8_6_368/ChangeLog">1</a>, <a href="http://svn.ruby-lang.org/repos/ruby/tags/v1_8_7_160/ChangeLog">2</a>).</p>
<p>The released tarballs are available at:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.6-p368.tar.gz">ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.6-p368.tar.gz</a></li>
<li><a href="ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.6-p368.tar.bz2">ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.6-p368.tar.bz2</a></li>
<li><a href="ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.6-p368.zip">ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.6-p368.zip</a></li>
<li><a href="ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.7-p160.tar.gz">ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.7-p160.tar.gz</a></li>
<li><a href="ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.7-p160.tar.bz2">ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.7-p160.tar.bz2</a></li>
<li><a href="ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.7-p160.zip">ftp://ftp.ruby-lang.org/pub/ruby/1.8/ruby-1.8.7-p160.zip</a></li>
</ul>
</blockquote>
<p>These updates are only worth pursuing if it's of utmost importance that you have the latest point release of your chosen Ruby version installed - in critical production environments, perhaps. For your development machine, it's more of a take it or leave it deal. I'll be sticking to the Apple supplied version of 1.8.6 (p114) for now.</p>
