<p>Jonathan Weiss recently had <a href="http://blog.innerewut.de/2008/1/3/24c3-ruby-on-rails-security">a session about Ruby on Rails security</a> at 24C3, the 24th Chaos Communication Congress, in Germany. He has made the slides (44 of them) available, as well as a 45 minute long Google Video with the slides, video, and sound to enjoy. Jonathan goes into data sanitation, cross site scripting attacks, cookies, and lots of similarly good stuff.</p>
