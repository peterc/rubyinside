Rather than spend $3049 on <a href="http://www.macromates.com/">TextMate</a>, the thrifty Dr. Nic Williams decided to try and port all of TextMate's useful 'snippets' over to RadRails. <a href="http://drnicwilliams.com/2006/11/06/post-halloween-radrails-trick-all-textmate-snippets-available/">He has succeeded</a>. </p>
<p>This is great news for Linux and Windows based Ruby developers who want to get in on the macro-fun without switching to the Mac. 199 Ruby snippets and 48 HTML snippets have been ported across.</p>
