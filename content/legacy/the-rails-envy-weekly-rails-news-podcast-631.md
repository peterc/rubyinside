<p>"Those <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/">Rails Envy</a> guys from the <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/tags/Commercials">Rails Vs Whatever</a> commercials" are back again with a new project. Called, simply, the <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/podcast">Rails Envy Podcast</a>, Jason Seifer and Gregg Pollack discuss the weekly happenings in the Rails world. Taking the completely opposite approach to the <a href="http://podcast.rubyonrails.org/">Rails Podcast</a>, with its interviews and indepth discussions, the Rails Envy Podcast flies through Rails' headline news in just several minutes. They're already on episode 2 and are publishing a new episode each Tuesday. The <a href="http://www.pragmaticstudio.com/">Pragmatic Studio</a> sponsors the show.</p>
<p>As an aside, they're also selling all of the <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/2007/10/9/ruby-on-rails-vs-net-2">Rails vs Whatever commercials on DVD</a> and also have a new <a href="http://www.railsenvy.com/2007/10/9/ruby-on-rails-vs-net-2">Rails vs .NET commercial</a> to show.</p>
