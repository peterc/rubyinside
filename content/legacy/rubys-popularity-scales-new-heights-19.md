<p>The creator of Ruby on Rails, David Heinemeier Hansson, has <a href="http://www.loudthinking.com/arc/000590.html">made the cover of Linux Journal</a>. Of more interest is their impressive dedication to Ruby topics this month, another sure sign that Ruby is on the way up.</p>
<p>The nay-sayers have dismissed Ruby in the past, but if it isn't higher than Python on the <a href="http://www.tiobe.com/tpci.htm">TIOBE Programming Community Index</a> (popularity of different programming languages) by May 2008, I'll eat my hat. Ruby is currently at #20 and Python is at #8.</p>
