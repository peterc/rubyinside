<p><a href="http://junior.tesly.com/">Tesly Jr.</a> is a service that provides good-looking HTML reports for your tests. It can hook into Ruby / Rails' <em>Test::Unit</em> library and receive the results of tests as they're being completing. The results are logged and shown on the Tesly Jr. site, allowing your customers to see up to date results as you go. You can also subscribe to RSS feeds that are updated every time your tests are run.</p>
