<p>O'Reilly has just released "<a href="http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/rjsrails/">RJS Template for Rails</a>" by <a href="http://www.codyfauser.com/">Cody Fauser</a>, the god of RJS templates. RJS templates are used in Ruby to create a full AJAX experience. They let you adjust and add elements onto the current page without reloading by using nice, clean Ruby code, without getting down and dirty with JavaScript.</p>
<p>I've just bought the book and it's 58 pages of goodness. I don't work with RJS too often, so this guide is already opening my eyes to my techniques I wasn't aware of before. If you're developing Rails apps and aren't intimately familiar with RJS, you need it.</p>
