<p><a href="http://www.hotscripts.com/">HotScripts.com</a>, a popular destination for finding PHP and CGI scripts, has added <a href="http://www.hotscripts.com/Ruby_on_Rails/index.html">a Ruby on Rails category</a>. Currently only 30 resources are listed, but as one of the heavyweights in the Web developer resources industry, it's likely to get a lot bigger soon. If you're a developer you might want to get your stuff added while it's still small.</p>
