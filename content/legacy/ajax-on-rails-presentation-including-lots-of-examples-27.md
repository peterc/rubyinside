<p>Stuart Halloway and Justin Gehtland of Relevance LLC have put together an online presentation called <a href="http://www.codecite.com/presentation/ruby/rails/ajax_rails">AJAX on Rails</a>. It looks at AJAX, its relation to Rails, how Prototype works, and provides lots of great code examples. If you want to brush up on your whole AJAX and Rails knowledge, it's worth looking at. A <a href="http://www.codecite.com/print/pres/ruby/rails/ajax_rails">PDF version</a> is also available.</p>
