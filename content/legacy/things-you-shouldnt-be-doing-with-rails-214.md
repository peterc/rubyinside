<p>Kevin Clark presents "<a href="http://glu.ttono.us/articles/2006/08/30/guide-things-you-shouldnt-be-doing-in-rails">Things You Shouldn't Be Doing in Rails</a>". It's not a list of things Rails isn't any good for, rather it's <em>features or concepts related to Rails</em> that you shouldn't be using.  It covers deprecated finders, the in-built pagination classes, scaffolding, engines, layouts, and namespaced controllers.</p>
