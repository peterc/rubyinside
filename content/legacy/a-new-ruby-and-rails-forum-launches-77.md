<p>A couple of enterprising Ruby developers have put together a forum for Ruby and Rails developers called <a href="http://railsforum.com/">Rails Forum</a> (<em>railsforum.com</em>). It's very new, but the posts seem to be trickling through already. As with most forums, it's a chicken and egg situation, but if you enjoy forums, consider signing up and contributing. It's time the community had a large, bustling forum, as some newcomers find them extremely valuable.</p>
