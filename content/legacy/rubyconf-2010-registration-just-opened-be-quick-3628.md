<p><a href="http://rubyconf.org/pages/register">Early bird registration</a> for <a href="http://rubyconf.org/">RubyConf 2010</a> - to be held in New Orleans November 11-13 - has just opened and you need to get in <em>fast</em> if you want to go. RubyConf has a reputation for selling out <em>very</em> quickly and even though some waiting list folks usually make it in, if you're dead set on going, buy your tickets immediately. They're $300.</p>
<p>Not got a second to waste? Go <em>direct</em> to <a href="http://rubyconf.stagehq.com/events/310/booking/new">the ticket ordering page by clicking here.</a></p>
<p><em>Note: I don't get a cut of ticket sales or even get asked to post about RubyConf. I just remember reading a torrent of endless misery on Twitter when it sold out so quickly last year ;-)</em></p>
