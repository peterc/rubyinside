<p>NetBeans developer, Tor Norbye, <a href="http://blogs.sun.com/tor/entry/ruby_screenshot_of_the_week6">continues to tease the Ruby community</a> with his series of screenshots showing Ruby (and Rails) support in the latest development builds of NetBeans. This week he announces the release of a "Ruby-only slimmed down version of NetBeans" and <a href="http://blogs.sun.com/tor/entry/ruby_screenshot_of_the_week6">shows off</a> some compelling new features such as the ability to run Rake tasks from NetBeans, a database migration tool, and Mongrel support.</p>
