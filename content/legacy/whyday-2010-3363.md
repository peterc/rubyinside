<blockquote class="stylized"><p>This year, on August 19, celebrate Whyday. Set aside that day to remember Why's contributions to our community and culture by hacking just for the fun and joy of it.</p></blockquote>
<p><cite>Glenn Vanderburg</cite></p>
<p>This is a great idea. It's still a couple of months way, but on <a href="http://whyday.org/">whyday.org</a> you can sign up for more updates, or you can follow <a href="http://twitter.com/celebratewhyday">@celebratewhyday</a> on Twitter.</p>
<p>If you want to reminisce in the meantime, Ruby Inside's link-packed <a href="why-the-lucky-stiff-links-2333.html">A Cup Full of Why: 32 Why The Lucky Stiff Links</a> will get you started.</p>
