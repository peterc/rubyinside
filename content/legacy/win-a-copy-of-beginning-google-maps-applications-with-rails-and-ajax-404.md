<p><a href="http://googlemapsbook.com/">Beginning Google Maps Applications with Rails and Ajax</a> is a book by Andre Lewis, Michael Purvis, Jeffrey Sambells, and Cameron Turner that, simply, looks at how to develop Google Maps powered applications using Rails. You can <a href="http://googlemapsbook.com/book/">read some sample chapters</a> from the very-similar PHP edition (sorry!) on the official site.<br>
The authors are running a free prize draw for at least 3 free copies of the book, and entries must be in by midnight on March 14th Eastern Time. They call it a 'contest' but you don't have to actually do anything other than fill in your name and e-mail address, and they actively support you passing the link around so all your friends can enter too (unless they happen to work for Apress).</p>
