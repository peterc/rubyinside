<p>Russ Olsen has been putting together a small series of blog posts about building a DSL in Ruby. The <a href="http://jroller.com/page/rolsen?entry=building_a_dsl_in_ruby">first part</a> dates from October 2006, but the <a href="http://jroller.com/page/rolsen?entry=building_a_dsl_in_ruby1">second part</a> has just been released, making it a good double length read.
