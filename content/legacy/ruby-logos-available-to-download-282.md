<p>The <a href="http://rubyidentity.org/">Ruby Visual Identity Team</a> have released a <a href="http://rubyidentity.org/ruby-logo-kit.zip">Ruby logo kit</a> for use under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike license. The kit includes high resolution JPEG and PNG bitmaps, along with PDF, Adobe Illustrator, Xara, and Flash vector format versions. Subject to the Creative Commons license, this logo pack makes it easy to use the official Ruby logo within your own projects and Web sites (perhaps a redesign of Ruby Inside's logo should be on the cards..)</p>
