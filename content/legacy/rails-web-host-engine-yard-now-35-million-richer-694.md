<p>Prominent Rails Web hosting company <a href="http://benchmark.com/news/sv/2008/01_11_2008.php">Engine Yard</a> have raised $3.5 million from Benchmark Capital, a particularly successful venture capital firm who backed AOL and eBay in the early days. Money is afloweth into the Ruby and Rails sector!</p>
<p>The <a href="http://www.infoq.com/news/2008/01/engine-yard-gets-three-five">best write up of this news</a> is by Obie Fernandez over at InfoQ. He talks about Engine Yard's operation, their hiring of Rubinius developers, and Engine Yard's thoughts for the future. Interestingly:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>In discussing the benefits of taking VC money at this time, Engine Yard CEO Lance emphasized the fact that unlike hosting, Rubinius development is a longer-term play, which along with Merb may eventually become part of a commercially-available technology stack.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Nice work, Engine Yard. The drinks are on you!</p>
