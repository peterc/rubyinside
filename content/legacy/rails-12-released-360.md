<p>No editorial on this, as I want to get it out quick, and I know some readers aren't subscribers to the official Rails blog.. but.. <a href="http://weblog.rubyonrails.com/2007/1/19/rails-1-2-rest-admiration-http-lovefest-and-utf-8-celebrations">Rails 1.2 has just been released!</a> Or, more accurately, Rails 1.2.1, as the obligatory bug-fix release followed on a little more quickly than usual :) You might want to get it straight away before the servers melt down with the rush tomorrow. Just use <strong><em>gem install rails --include-dependencies</em></strong><em> </em>as usual.</p>
<p>David Heinemeier Hansson has put together a <a href="http://weblog.rubyonrails.com/2007/1/19/rails-1-2-rest-admiration-http-lovefest-and-utf-8-celebrations">great, info packed post</a> about what to expect and looks at some of the new features.</p>
