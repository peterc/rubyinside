<p>Pat Eyler of O'Reilly's Ruby blog has <a href="http://www.oreillynet.com/ruby/blog/2006/06/rubyquestions_for_the_rubyquiz.html">interviewed James Gray of Ruby Quiz fame</a>. James talks about how he was won over to Ruby via Perl 6, and how the Ruby Quiz has continued to explode in popularity.</p>
<p><em>(If you haven't checked it out, the </em><em><a href="http://www.rubyquiz.com/">Ruby Quiz</a></em><em> is a weekly Ruby programming contest. A new challenge is issued each Friday, and a few days later everyone chips in their solutions and ideas. All of this is then archived on the site, allowing anyone to learn some great Ruby tricks whenever they like. There are currently 82 quizzes to learn about, so </em><em><a href="http://www.rubyquiz.com/">enjoy</a></em><em>.)</em></p>
