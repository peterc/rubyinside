<p><a href="http://www.regonline.com/Checkin.asp?EventId=636795"><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1168" style="float: left; margin: 0px 12px 12px 0px;" src="/assets/2008/09/rubyconflogo-300x85.jpg" alt="" width="200"></a>Registration for <a href="http://rubyconf.org/">RubyConf 2008</a> is now open.  The <a href="http://rubyconf.org/talks">schedule</a> consists of three heavily packed days of presentations, with three separate talks taking place at any given time.</p>
<p>Noteworthy this year is an all day Ruby Implementors' track that boasts a series of talks covering all the major Ruby VM implementations, a keynote by Dave Thomas, a Q&amp;A with Matz, and the always popular random assortment of Lightning Talks.</p>
<p>Chad Fowler <a href="http://www.chadfowler.com/2008/9/10/rubyconf-registration-is-open">notes</a> that past RubyConfs have sold all 500 seats within 4 hours of opening registration, so be sure to register soon.</p>
