<p><a href="http://nullstyle.com/2007/5/9/erlectricity-hi-ruby-i-m-erlang">Erlectricity</a> is a very early stage library in a "pre-release" stage that acts as an interoperability bridge between Ruby and Erlang processes. Scott Fleckenstein is the developer, and he is going to continue blogging about Erlectricity's development, and Ruby / Erlang interoperability on <a href="http://nullstyle.com/">his blog</a>. While this topic isn't quite mainstream yet, <a href="http://groups.google.com/group/ruby-talk-google/browse_thread/thread/ad3c5d7527c62cce">many</a> Ruby developers have become interested in <a href="http://www.erlang.org/">Erlang</a> lately and Ruby / Erlang interoperability and co-operation is likely to become a big topic in the Ruby community towards the end of the year.</p>
