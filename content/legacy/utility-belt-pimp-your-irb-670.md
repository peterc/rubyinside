<p><a href="http://utilitybelt.rubyforge.org/">Utility Belt</a> bills itself as "a grab-bag of tricks, tools, techniques, trifles, and toys for IRB," the interactive Ruby shell. If you're a keen IRB user, Utility Belt offers up a metric ton of features that will promote you to power user in no time including interaction with your favorite text editor, clipboard support, Pastie posting, Amazon S3 shortcuts, auto-indentation, and more. Utility Belt appears to be optimized somewhat for OS X but most of the features will work on other platforms too.</p>
