<p>Registration for Texas's own Ruby conference, the <a href="http://lonestarrubyconf.com/index.html">Lone Star Ruby Conference</a>, has <a href="http://lonestarrubyconf.com/registration.html">opened</a>. The overall event takes place between September 4 - 6, 2008 in the Norris Conference Center in Austin, TX. Tickets are $250 for the two-day conference portion, or $425 for one day of training / tutorials followed by the two day conference. The prices shoot up come August 10.</p>
<p>The <a href="http://lonestarrubyconf.com/program.html">schedule</a> is bumper packed with some great <a href="http://lonestarrubyconf.com/speakers.html">sessions and tutorials</a>. James Edward Gray II and Gregory Brown will be delivering a three hour training session on Ruby's IO functionality, Jim Weirich and Joe O'Brien will be delivering a tutorial on Test Driven Development, Gregg Pollack and Jason Seifer (of RailsEnvy fame) will be giving an advanced ActiveRecord workshop, and lots more besides. This seems to be one of those rare events where the tutorial day will be worth its weight in gold, unless you're already a self-certified Ruby / Rails expert of course. As well as this, Yehuda Katz, Glenn Vanderburg, Evan Phoenix, Matz, Bruce Tate, Bruce Williams, and others, will be speaking on <a href="http://lonestarrubyconf.com/program.html">various Ruby topics</a> at the conference generally.</p>
