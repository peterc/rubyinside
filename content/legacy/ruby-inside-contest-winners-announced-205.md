<p>The <a href="final-entries-in-the-ruby-inside-100-15-contest-204.html">entries and the numbers associated with them</a> were shown in the last post, so choosing a winner is as simple as using 'rand'. For the first prize $100 drawing:</p>
<pre>irb(main):001:0&gt; rand(24) + 1
=&gt; 19
</pre>
<p>1st prize of $100 goes to Duncan Beevers with <a href="http://duncan.beevers.net/?p=41">Using Simile Timeline with Ruby on Rails Views</a>. A well deserved win too, as it's a particularly cool demonstration of how to use a JavaScript / AJAX library to show timelines on your Web pages.</p>
<p>And for the second drawing:</p>
<pre>irb(main):002:0&gt; rand(6) + 1
=&gt; 3
</pre>
<p>2nd prize of $15 goes to <a href="http://timshadel.com/2006/08/18/the-100-contest-for-ruby-blogging">Tim Shadel.</a></p>
<p>Thanks for all of the entries folks, and I hope those of you who didn't win will still enjoy checking out <a href="final-entries-in-the-ruby-inside-100-15-contest-204.html">the 24 great articles and resources created for the contest.</a></p>
