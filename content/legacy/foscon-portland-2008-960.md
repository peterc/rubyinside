<p><a href="http://pdxfoscon.org/start">FOSCON</a> is a "free, fun gathering" of Ruby developers held alongside the O'Reilly OSCON conference currently taking place in Portland, Oregon. It's tomorrow (Wednesday, July 23) at <a href="http://pdxfoscon.org/venue">CubeSpace</a> (near the Oregon Convention Center) from 6pm. The always proactive <a href="http://pdxruby.org/">Portland Ruby Brigade</a> have organized it, and a surprising number of interesting presentations are already planned, including one on IronRuby from John Lam of Microsoft. A live coding competition will also be taking place.</p>
