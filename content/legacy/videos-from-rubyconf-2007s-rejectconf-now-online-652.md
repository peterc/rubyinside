<p>Just learned via <a href="http://twitter.com/wdperson">Michael Riley</a> on Twitter that the <a href="http://rejectconf4.confreaks.com/">videos from the 4th RejectConf</a> held alongside <a href="http://rubyconf.org/">RubyConf 2007</a> are available to watch online. There are plenty of good presentations to watch, such as Dr. Nic Williams' <a href="http://rejectconf4.confreaks.com/d2t3_4th_annual_reject_conf_magic_controller.html">introduction to Rubigen and Magic Controller</a>, Tom Preston <a href="http://rejectconf4.confreaks.com/d2t3_4th_annual_reject_conf_god.html">talking about GOD</a>, and Eric Mills on <a href="http://rejectconf4.confreaks.com/d2t3_4th_annual_reject_conf_rubedo.html">rubedo - a Ruby powered jukebox</a>. There are <a href="http://rejectconf4.confreaks.com/">20 different presentations</a> you can watch (including Ryan Davis' introduction) so there's bound to be something you'll find interesting. Being RubyConf's RejectConf, the presentations are nearly all about Ruby-specific topics, rather than Rails.</p>
