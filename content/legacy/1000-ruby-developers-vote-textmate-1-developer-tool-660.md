<p>Tim Bray presents "<a href="http://www.tbray.org/ongoing/When/200x/2007/11/26/Ruby-Tool-Survey">Ruby Survey Results,</a>" the results of a survey of 1000 Ruby developers on their favorite development tools, primarily focusing on editors / IDEs. In the category of Rails-focused developers, 38% of the vote went to <span style="font-style: italic;"><a href="http://macromates.com/">TextMate</a></span>, with <span style="font-style: italic;">vi</span>, <span style="font-style: italic;">NetBeans</span> and <span style="font-style: italic;">Eclipse</span> lagging far behind. With Ruby-focused developers, however, <span style="font-style: italic;">vi</span> edged out <span style="font-style: italic;">TextMate</span> by a single vote, with <span style="font-style: italic;">TextMate</span>, <span style="font-style: italic;">Emacs</span> and <span style="font-style: italic;">Netbeans</span> as runners up. With all results tallied together, however, <span style="font-style: italic;">TextMate</span> took a healthy lead on vi, and cements its position as the #1 preferred development tool of Ruby developers overall.</p>
