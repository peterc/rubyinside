<p><a href="http://gemjack.com/">GemJack.com</a> is a basic, but highly effective, site featuring full HTML documentation for most of the Ruby gems (Ruby's library packaging system). Hopefully it'll get indexed by Google in full and beginners will find it easier to find the documentation they want straight from Google.</p>
<p>One alternative, however, is to run <em>gem_server</em> on your local machine, whereupon all your installed gems' documentation will be available at <em>http://localhost:8808/</em> . If your gems don't have documentation pre-built, you can build it with <em>gem rdoc -all</em><br>
