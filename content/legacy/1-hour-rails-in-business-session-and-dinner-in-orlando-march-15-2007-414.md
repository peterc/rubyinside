<p>Robert Dempsey, Founder of <a href="http://www.railsforall.org/">Rails For All</a>, Inc. (a new Rails advocacy group), and Gregg Pollack, CTO of ADS, will be <a href="http://www.orug.org/articles/2007/02/21/next-orug-meeting">presenting a one-hour session</a> for managers and developers at DeVry University's Orlando campus on Thursday, March 15 as part of the <a href="http://www.orug.org/">Orlando Ruby Group</a>. There will be a free dinner, provided by Rails For All, so they suggest you arrive hungry! They say the session will focus on the business advantages behind Ruby on Rails and is particularly ideal for managers as well as developers looking for work. More details about the session can be found <a href="http://www.orug.org/articles/2007/02/21/next-orug-meeting">here</a>.</p>
