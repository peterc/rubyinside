<p><a href="http://futureruby.com/"><img src="/assets/2009/03/futureruby.png" width="137" height="87" alt="futureruby.png" style="float:left; margin-right:12px; margin-bottom:12px; border:1px #000000 solid;"></a> 2008's <a href="rubyfringe-success-and-roundup-956.html">RubyFringe conference</a>, put on by Canadian Rails consultancy <a href="http://unspace.ca/">Unspace</a>, was heralded as a landmark in Ruby conferences and set a benchmark that has only recently been approached by the fervor around events like <a href="http://rubymanor.org/">Ruby Manor</a>. After the event, Unspace rapidly said that there wouldn't be another RubyFringe, but...</p>
<p>Unspace has decided to <a href="http://rethink.unspace.ca/2009/2/19/future-ruby">do it all again with FutureRuby</a>. Like RubyFringe, <a href="http://futureruby.com/">FutureRuby</a> is a single track, small (150 attendees max) conference with a focus on high energy, high quality presentations (see <a href="the-rubyfringe-videos-vids-from-the-summers-hottest-conference-1214.html">some videos from RubyFringe</a>, and remember that Giles Bowkett was named <a href="top-ruby-presenter-of-2008-giles-bowkett-1425.html">our top presenter of 2008</a> for his RubyFringe show!) and lots of extracurricular activities (i.e. parties and other entertainment), swag, and festivity.</p>
<p>If you're interested but not sure, you can keep up with news about the event on <a href="http://twitter.com/futureruby">Twitter @futureruby</a> - I expect they will mention when they're about to run out of tickets on there, as well as post info about speakers, etc.</p>
<h3>Also.. The Ruby Hoedown Needs Your Help!</h3>
<p>Jeremy McAnally <a href="http://omgbloglol.com/help-make-a-free-ruby-conferen">is seeking to raise $15,000 from folks</a> who want to attend the next Ruby Hoedown (a very successful Nashville-based unconference in the past). Basically he wants the event to be "free" (barring the donations, of course!) with no sponsors, and $15,000 is needed to get a venue for 100s of people. If you've enjoyed the Hoedown in the past or just want to go to a great Ruby event in Tennessee this year, <a href="http://omgbloglol.com/help-make-a-free-ruby-conferen">see if you can help out</a>.</p>
