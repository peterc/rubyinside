<p>Prolific <a href="http://www.puneruby.com/blog/">PuneRuby</a> blogger Satish Talim has just begun a course of free Ruby lessons. They're open to anyone who wants to get involved, and so far 43 people are signed up! The lessons have already begun, but you can still get in on the action. The <a href="http://www.puneruby.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=33">syllabus is available to check out</a>, and to register to receive the lessons you only have to e-mail Satish at <strong>satish.talim /at gmail.com</strong>. This might be an ideal chance to get those friends who keep asking you questions about Ruby off your back ;-)</p>
